Tapestry

About

Tapestry is a STEM support group for young women who are underrepresented and in minority communities.

The vision is to focus on mental health by creating a positive virtual community.

We found that there are tons of resources to learn and gain new skills in tech, but there is a big gap between learning and how it can affect our mental health.

More and more young females are not pursuing a path or worse even dropping out of their major because of imposter syndrome.

At Tapestry, we want to enhance the experience to those on the college or bootcamp path by lending a hand of support - mental support that is.

Just like the name, this group will come together.

Mission

The idea behind Tapestry is to partner with college/universities along with coding bootcamps to better connect with students.

Our approach is zoning in on mental health practices such as cognitive therapy.

By joining, young women will get the chance to meet, engage, and express emotions on their STEM journey.

The virtual group will not only form as a sharing circle, but also host fun events that improves mental health.
